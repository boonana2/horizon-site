Poly Track

A fast-paced racing game where players race through custom tracks, compete for the fastest times, and build their own courses.

Knock your opponent off the platform to take their stock — the more damage they've taken, the farther they fly. Land hits to rack up damage percent, then finish with a strong knockback to send them off the edge. Play locally with a friend or test yourself against the CPU. First fighter to lose all three stocks loses the match.
